Cross-link conventions used

  • Concept pages at the wiki root: linked as [name](name.md).
  • Repo pages in repos/: linked from root as [name](repos/name.md), linked from inside repos/ as [name](name.md) for siblings or [../name](../name.md) for parent concepts.
  • Every page has a "Related" section at the bottom with 3-5 cross-references.
  • Every concept page references at least one specific repo + at least one specific lecture (via zero-to-hero-arc.md).
  • Every repo page references the architectural concepts it instantiates.
